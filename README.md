# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project consists of three interactive Power BI dashboards developed using an E-Commerce Sales dataset. The dashboards provide a comprehensive analysis of sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, future sales forecasting, and market basket analysis.

The primary objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that help organizations make data-driven decisions.

# 🚀 Dashboards Included

1. Sales Dashboard
2. Sales Forecast Dashboard (15 Days)
3. Market Basket Analysis Dashboard

# 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

Power BI Desktop
* Microsoft Excel (Data Source)
* Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Power BI Forecasting
* Bing Maps Visualization

# 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains E-Commerce transactions from 2019–2020 including:

* Order Details

* Product Categories
* Sub-Categories
* Sales

* Region

# 📈 Dashboard 2: Sales Forecast Dashboard

## Overview

This dashboard predicts future sales using Power BI Forecasting.

The forecasting model estimates sales for the next **15 days**, allowing businesses to plan inventory and sales strategies proactively.

## Visualizations

### Historical Sales Trend

Displays historical order-wise sales from:

* 2019
* 2020

### 15-Day Forecast

Uses Power BI's built-in forecasting feature to estimate future sales.

Forecast includes:

* Predicted Sales
* Confidence Interval
* Historical Trend

### Date Slicer

Allows users to:

* Zoom into a specific period
* Analyze custom date ranges

## Forecasting Features

# 🛒 Dashboard 3: Market Basket Analysis

## Overview

This dashboard analyzes purchasing behavior by identifying products that are frequently bought together.

It helps businesses understand customer buying patterns and optimize cross-selling opportunities.

## Visualizations

### Product List

Displays all available products within the dataset.

Users can search and filter products.

### Frequently Purchased Products

Shows products with the highest purchase frequency.

Examples include:

* Easy-Staple Paper
* Staples
* Staple Envelope
* Storage Products

## Business Applications

### Cross-Selling

Recommend complementary products.

Example:

Customers buying **Staples** can also be recommended **Staple Removers**.

### Product Placement

Arrange frequently purchased products closer together.

### Inventory Management

Maintain sufficient stock for frequently purchased items.

### Promotional Campaigns

Create bundle offers for commonly purchased products.

# 📈 Key Business Insights

## Sales Performance

* Office Supplies generated the highest overall sales.
* Standard Class is the most frequently used shipping mode.
* Consumer segment contributes the largest share of total sales.
* COD is the most preferred payment mode.

## Regional Analysis

* Sales performance varies significantly across states.
* Certain cities consistently generate higher revenue.
* Regional filters enable localized business analysis.

## Time-Based Analysis

* Monthly sales and profit trends reveal seasonal demand patterns.
* Comparison between 2019 and 2020 helps evaluate business growth.

## Product Performance

* Office Supplies dominate overall sales.
* Product-level analysis identifies best-selling items.
* Supports inventory optimization and pricing strategies.

## Forecast Insights

* Predicts future sales for the next 15 days.
* Confidence intervals provide an estimate of forecast reliability.
* Assists in operational and financial planning.

## Customer Behavior

* Consumer segment contributes the highest revenue.
* Customer payment preferences indicate strong adoption of Cash on Delivery.
* Market Basket Analysis highlights products frequently purchased together.
